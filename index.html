FAQ

Helpful answers before you begin.

When can I start postpartum yoga?

Start only after your doctor or physiotherapist clears you for movement. The practice can then be adapted to your recovery stage.

Can this work after a C-section?

Yes, with careful modifications, medical clearance, and a slower progression around scar comfort, core pressure, and fatigue.
